First attempt of YKI and the result is very surprising! 🌸
My experience on it:
1. Studying with a professional teacher regularly over years, so the foundation is rather good. 
In reading/speaking/listening parts, even though I’ve never done any exam practice, I didn’t find it hard to handle; 
2. Myself summed up a writing template that can apply to all topics. 😁
In total there’re 12 sentences, which only requires 8-9 extra sentences about my own opinions. This makes the preparation much easier. 
Besides I put 3-5 key words and sentences for each topic, so only memorizing before the exam. 
Writing is my weakest subject, and I didn’t practice for any specific topic at all(not enough time nor patience🫠🫠). It’s surprising how well the template helped. 
This is untraditional as how people usually prepare for it. But I’m happy for the result :)) in total I studied 17h for the exam. Three days intensive studies. 
And for further questions, yes I will continue studying with teacher, as we’re also practicing daily advanced language topics, which helps a lot of my life here. I don’t wanna be a “Finnish citizen” without speaking Finnish. 
Also, if you’re interested in the templates and other materials I sorted, I can share, with some fee we can discuss. 
In this way, it won’t be an open material that later on everyone is using, which ensures the authencity  to pass the exam.
